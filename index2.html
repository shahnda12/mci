Types of graphic design

Types of graphic design: There are different types of graphic designs, and all of them fall under one name, in which the client's need and the idea to be communicated to him are considered. The types of graphic design are summarized as follows:

advertising design: Graphic design is created in the field of advertising to give way to highlight a certain thing in the design to suit the main goal of the advertisement to make it shine in the eyes of people when they look at it.

Web interface design: Graphic design is used in the interfaces of websites as it gives your website a special touch mixed with the spirit of creativity to make the viewer feel comfortable and calm when browsing within your site.

Social Media Posts: Graphic design is included in social media publications to increase the ability to draw the viewer's attention to the products or services offered by your company, and also works well to increase awareness of your brand by setting specific standards for publication and design on the social media platforms of a company, to leave an unforgettable impact on everyone visits your pages. In a market crowded with millions of posts per day, professional graphic design is your ultimate tool to stand out and make a good impression on your potential clients.

visual identity design Graphic design is a direct link between the end customer and the owners of services and products, and it is an indispensable part for most advertisers and business owners, as graphic design is the primary tool for creating a visual identity and instilling it in the minds of viewers. One of the most important of these applications is the design of brands for companies, institutions or even individuals. The visual identity consists of many parts and stages, the most important of which are: * Study the brand and its philosophy and how this philosophy will affect the visual identity of the company * Color spectra and the philosophy of colors and their use in various publications for visual identity * Fonts used in visual identity * Visual identity applications such as personal cards, official correspondence paper, message envelopes, marketing flags, and many other accessories with which we can enhance the visual identity of a company.

Packaging Designs Packaging design is included in everything we use in our lives. It depends on defining products and highlighting their features to draw the customer's attention and motivate him to buy. It is a good option for brand awareness and customer loyalty.

Publications Design This type of designs is the most famous, as the content writer and designer are coordinated to focus on a specific aspect of the design, and then it is printed on newspaper publications, but this type is no longer of great importance today, as many people prefer to publish on social media. social media instead of newspapers and magazines.

motion graphics It is animated illustrations. These graphics include pictures, animation graphs, and charts. Music, sound effects, and voiceover are usually added to such videos, and this type is usually used to convey a specific idea that is difficult to communicate with still images.

Environmental design denotes many fields and applied sciences that deal with creating the human-designed environment. These fields include architecture, geography, urban planning, landscape architecture, and interior design.
